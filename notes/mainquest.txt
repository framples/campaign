## OVERVIEW OF MAIN QUEST

What: 
Aleister requires assistance of 4 esteemed trackers/fighters/hunters/warriors to find both of his sons.  The group must return them alive to their father if possible for questioning on the whereabouts of their sister is.  If force is required, so-be-it. 

Why: 
Youngest daughter is missing and Aleister is certain they are both behind the disappearance. 

Reward: The members of the group will each get an epic mount, gold, free passage on whitlock ships for life, and the gratitude of one of the most longstanding families in the country.  Favors and good words go a long way from the Whitlocks.
